for Laura

Laura, these words are for you to keep
A poem born from thoughts that wouldn't rest
Some feelings run too deep to sleep
So I wrote them down, my best way to be honest

From heaven to earth, no matter how far I go
You're my heaven, no matter the cost
This distance makes the days feel slow
But nothing about this feeling's lost

This feeling for you just keeps on growing
Stronger with each passing day
Every day together, it's still showing
How deep this love has come to stay

Walk every street, let the sun kiss your face
Take in the waves, unwind and go slow
Let this trip be yours, your own kind of grace
Enjoy every moment, at your own flow

The tide always returns to the shore
And I know that you'll come back too
So enjoy your sun, your sea, explore
I'll be right here, waiting for you

So this is my heart, put into rhyme
Missing you more than words can start
Go live this trip, take all your time
I'll be right here for my sweetheart
